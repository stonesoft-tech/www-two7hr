With our extensive experience, we can help with any employment issue. two 7 will guide you through the complex world of HR processes and associated employment law, whether you are looking for advice/support with HR basics, developing employee policies, succession planning, working on exit strategies, handling difficult internal decisions and everything in between, we are here to help.


Contact Us

Whilst we work to your requirements, some of the services we offer are:

Emerging Companies

Preparation of employment contracts, staff handbooks, people policies and procedures, recruitment and answering day-to-day HR queries.

Established Companies

We work with senior management to improve employee engagement and productivity, assist with performance review, talent management/development, succession planning and deal with issues associated with growth and change.

Growing Companies

Revision and preparation of employment contracts, recruitment and answering day-to-day HR queries. Reviewing existing staff handbooks, people policies and procedures. Coaching of team leaders in areas such as managing performance, productivity, managing absence and staff development.

HR Support Packages

Companies that don't necessarily need an in-house HR person, but do need support for an existing HR function are able to access one of our support packages. Support can be provided remotely or on-site; for as much or as little time as you need.

HR Project Work

You may have specific issues – for example a redundancy situation, complex people issues, hybrid / remote working, or assistance with restructuring. We will work on specific projects with aims and outcomes defined at the beginning, working within an agreed timescale, budget, and terms.

Mergers and Acquisitions

TUPE (Transfer of Undertakings (Protection of Employees) the law that protects employees if the business they work for joins another or is sold) is complex and challenging to navigate. Managing the transfer correctly requires a skilled understanding. We can consult on TUPE processes and make the transition easy.
